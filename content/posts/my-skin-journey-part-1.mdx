Hello lovelies, today I'm gonna be addressing a battle that I have been going through for YEARSSSSS. My skin. I have been struggling with acne on and off since I was 13. After I got my period at age 11 I slowly started developing a few pimples here and there. Thoughtlessly, I bought a random clean and clear face wash not knowing about any of the ingredients and slapped it onto my face. Obviously my skin barrier suffered a lot and this act launched my longggggggggg acne journey.

circa nov 2020 age 13

All of a sudden, I started getting acne on my cheeks. I was still using only drugstore products but hadn't really changed my regimen. I believe this was due to the constant mask wearing during school times combined with the destroyer of my skin barrier (overusing random drug store products).

THENNN guess what my skin decided to misbehave even more.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/a/AVvXsEj-ZJdGzZlWmiVYl2OOW6Z7atZvZygeGW0N1_rfcKrAsLNGyGg3V5LU6BvHNH8wMGNDb4ZxOnuMIovxHuTrdu7BBom4T1ZMhDWoAvAk3LtKmNwWoEKskRyfGn8rB2YGoa9_TYkRHCZZmWP7HdHz0BDMCBT_FHKtgCX1Nt1yoODcwVt6VXSR9LGvAzV4HG92)

I had finally had enough and decided to pay a visit to my local walk-in doctor. I was prescribed minocycline and a topical cream called TactuPump.
