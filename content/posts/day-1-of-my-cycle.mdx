Hello everyone, after a very long delay my period has finally started. After months of pretty consistent periods this is the first time in a while it is significantly late. Anyways without further ado lets start the post game interview. Day 1 was fine, usually my periods don't come with very many side effects physically, more so mentally. I found myself overthinking a bit more than normal but not anything debilitating. If anything I felt better then before my period started as during my luteal phase is usually when I feel my worst. In terms of my appetite, it significantly decreased from the days prior. I am no longer craving unhealthy foods and drinks. As goes for my motivation I have not really been practicing my current hobbies. Since I was expecting my period almost 10 days ago I've been feeling increasingly sluggish and less motivated. Although I do find today I am feeling a bit better. OH and one last thing, my skin has been breaking out a bit. Anyways girls see you tomorrow!
